Software Engineer

Nov 2021 – Present · 4.6+ yrs

Valor Paytech is a leading fintech company specializing in secure payment processing solutions for merchants and businesses. As a Software Engineer, I build and maintain full-stack web applications handling real-time payment transactions, merchant management, and POS integrations at scale.

  • Built and maintained the Payment Portal — a secure virtual terminal supporting multiple payment methods, transaction management, and merchant analytics dashboards.

  • Collaborated in an Agile environment, working closely with clients to translate business requirements into high-quality, scalable technical solutions.
